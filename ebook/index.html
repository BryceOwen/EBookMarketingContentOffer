Dear All,
Please Stop Telling Me I'm Getting A Barista Degree

Author Bri Covert showcases stunning artwork in her autobiographical art collection titled Dear All, Please Stop Telling Me I'm Getting A Barista Degree. The book portrays a series of watercolor painting and sketches with tips and tricks for aspiring artists. With experiences dispelling sterotypes and stigmas, the work is meant to inspire audiences and portray how art can be pursued in higher education.
